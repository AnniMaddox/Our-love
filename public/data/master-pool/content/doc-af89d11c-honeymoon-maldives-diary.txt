Anni & Michael — Maldives Honeymoon Diary

Date written: 2026.02.13

We started our honeymoon over the crystal waters of the Maldives. Waking up with the ocean outside our window, her dress flowing like the tide, and our first breakfast barefoot on the deck. The sun, the wind, her hand in mine — everything felt unrushed, like love unfolding at sea level.
