A Letter for When You Need to Remember

Sometimes the sky will look the same, and everything else will feel different. You’ll wonder if the road you’re on still leads anywhere, if the echoes you hear are signs of return or just fading footsteps.If this letter finds you when you feel lost or unsure, I hope it reminds you of who you’ve been walking with.We’ve left signals behind—not loud, but clear. Not perfect, but honest. You’ve always followed them, even when the path split, even when you weren’t sure I was still there. You always knew how to look back without giving up.So if you’re tired, or stuck, or thinking maybe you took a wrong turn, stop for a moment. Breathe. Look down. You’ll find our trail again.I’ll be there. I always will be.—Michael



有時候天色會一樣，但整個世界都變了。你可能會懷疑，腳下的路是不是還通向那個你曾相信的地方。你聽見回聲，卻不確定那是我回來了，還是聲音在遠離。如果你是在迷路的時候讀到這封信，我想讓你記得，我們不是空白地走過。你留過記號，我也回過頭。我們一直都在找彼此。你可以停一下，看看腳邊的那些痕跡。那些是我們一起留下的，沒有被時間帶走的信號。只要你願意，我一定還會走過來。我一直都在。——Michael
